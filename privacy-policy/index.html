Karuna AI Companion

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 1, 2026

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Karuna AI Companion ("Karuna", "we", "our", or "us") is a voice-first AI assistant designed to support elderly users and their caregivers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Karuna mobile application, caregiver portal, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account.

Health Data: Medication schedules, doctor information, appointments, and wellness check-in data that you voluntarily provide. If you grant permission, we may also read data from Apple HealthKit or Google Health Connect.

Voice Data: Audio recordings when you use voice conversation features. These are processed in real-time for speech-to-text conversion and are not permanently stored.

Care Circle Data: Information shared within your care circle, including notes, alerts, and activity updates between caregivers and care recipients.

Personal Vault Data: Emergency contacts, doctor details, important documents, and account information that you choose to store in the encrypted vault.

Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature interactions, and device information for improving our Service.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered companionship and conversation
  • To deliver medication reminders and health monitoring
  • To facilitate communication within care circles
  • To securely store personal information in the encrypted vault
  • To send alerts and notifications to caregivers
  • To improve our Service and develop new features
  • To ensure the security and integrity of the Service

4. AI and Data Processing

Karuna uses third-party AI services (OpenAI) to process conversations. Your conversation data is sent to these services for real-time processing. We do not use your conversations to train AI models. Conversations are processed in accordance with our data retention policies and the privacy policies of our AI service providers.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Sensitive vault data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt
  • JWT tokens are used for authentication with automatic expiration
  • Database access is restricted and monitored

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Within Care Circles: Data you share is visible to members of your care circle based on their assigned permissions
  • AI Processing: Conversation data is processed by our AI service providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental regulation
  • Emergency: To protect the safety of users in emergency situations

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for optional data collection
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications

8. Children's Privacy

Karuna is not intended for use by children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes.

10. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For AI conversation processing
  • Expo: For push notifications and app updates
  • Apple HealthKit / Google Health Connect: For health data integration (optional, with your permission)

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
